What is Rocket Legacy?

Rocket Legacy is the first idle arcade play-to-own game that has a multiplayer mode.
Players can admire our space fantasy setting and explore the game's economy through extracting resources, constructing buildings, creating items, defeating bosses, and visiting planets.

Rocket Legacy is Play-to-Own: the game is a universe full of planets that belong to players. Without any investment, you can:

  • play and own lands
  • invite other players to your planets or visit other players' planets to gather resources
  • defeat bosses together or challenge each other

You can find more information about Rocket Legacy in our White Paper:
https://rocket-legacy.gitbook.io/whitepaper

How to play on MacOS?

Press "Play now" button on the website. Once Rocket Legacy is downloaded, click on it, the interface will pop up. Press right click on the icon (or tap with two fingers on the trackpad) and choose "Open". Rocket Legacy launcher will appear and then press "Sign Up" button.

When will the Alpha be released? On which platforms will it be available?

The Alpha version is planned for early June 2025. The game will be available on Windows and MacOS.

You can download Rocket Legacy from the official website by clicking on "Play now" button.
